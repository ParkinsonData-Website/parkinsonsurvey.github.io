About

These tests are being conducted by members of the Hawaii Digital Health Lab working under Dr. Peter Washington, Assistant Professor of Information & Computer Sciences at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. The goal of these tests is to create a means of accessible, fair and free diagnosis for Parkinson's Disease. To do this, both participants suffering from the disease and healthy participants are being asked to complete a series of short tests, which will include moving a mouse in a specified direction and tapping specific keys when prompted. This test should take no more than 5 to 10 minutes of your time.

If there are any questions about the data collected or this website, please send an email to zawad@hawaii.edu
